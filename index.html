About Us

Amateur Radio Communications of the Philippines, Inc. (ARCOTP, Inc.)

Established on August 18, 2017, with the vision of fostering a thriving community of amateur radio enthusiasts in the Philippines. Founded by a group of passionate individuals who shared a common interest and love in radio communications. DX1ARC serves as a platform for learning, connection, and growth within the realm of amateur radio.

Vision

Making amateur radio fun, engaging, and relevant to the community.

Mission

To promote radio amateurism while having fun;

To serve the community's communication requirements especially during disasters and emergency;

To be at the forefront in the advancement of knowledge in different modes of radio communication;

To promote different outdoor and indoor amateur radio activities.

Core Values

At the heart of DX1ARC is a strong belief in the power of communication to bridge gaps, foster connections, and contribute to the dissemination of knowledge. The organization operates under a set of values that defined its culture and interactions:

    Committed: DX1ARC members are dedicated to the craft of amateur radio communications. They are committed to continuously improving their skills, fostering camaraderie among fellow enthusiasts, and promoting the importance of amateur radio as a means of communication and emergency preparedness.
    Organized: DX1ARC conducts its activities with structured coordination and efficient collaboration. Through the leadership of its officers and the active support of its members, the club is commited to meticulous planning, systematic approaches, and the seamless integration of individual efforts towards achieving collective goals.
    Ready: DX1ARC members are always prepared for the challenges and opportunities that amateur radio communications presented. They embrace new technologies, techniques, and advancements while ensuring they were well-prepared to contribute effectively to various events and activities.
    Excited: Enthusiasm and eagerness are always the driving force behind DX1ARC activities. The excitement to learn, share, and collaborate defined DX1ARC's culture. Members are always excited to explore new aspects of amateur radio, mentor newcomers, and participate in events that expanded their knowledge and skills.

ARCOTP, Inc's commitment to its values—Committed, Organized, Ready, and Excited—creates a culture that welcomes newcomers, fosters innovation, and brings together a diverse group of individuals sharing a common passion. ARCOTP, Inc. continues to engage in DX contests, DXpeditions, field days, and other events, its legacy lives on through the experiences and connections forged by its members.
