About Us

Welcome to the Radio Amateur Club! We are a community of radio enthusiasts who share a passion for amateur radio, communication, and learning. Join us to explore, connect, and grow together.

The Amateur Radio Communications of the Philippines, Incorporated is an amateur radio club based in the Philippines with the callsign, DX1ARC

Our home frequencies are at 144.760Hz and 431.950MHz.
